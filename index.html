ü•û Classic Pancakes

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg

Instructions:

  1. Mix all dry ingredients.
  2. Add milk and egg; whisk until smooth.
  3. Heat a pan and pour batter.
  4. Cook both sides until golden.
  5. Serve with syrup!

üçù Spaghetti Bolognese

Ingredients:

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g minced beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • Salt & pepper

Instructions:

  1. Boil spaghetti until tender.
  2. Fry onion & beef until brown.
  3. Add tomato sauce, salt & pepper.
  4. Mix sauce with spaghetti.
  5. Serve hot with cheese!

ü•ô Smocha (Samosa + Chapati)

Ingredients:

  • 1 Chapati
  • 1 Samosa
  • 1 tbsp Tomato sauce
  • 1 tbsp Chili sauce (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Warm the chapati on a pan.
  2. Place the samosa in the middle.
  3. Add tomato sauce (and chili if desired).
  4. Roll the chapati around the samosa tightly.
  5. Enjoy your Smocha!

üåΩ Mahindi Choma (Roasted Maize)

Ingredients:

  • 1 Fresh maize (corn on the cob)
  • 1/2 tsp Salt
  • 1 Lemon (optional, for squeezing)
  • Chili powder (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Peel back the husk of the maize (optional).
  2. Roast the maize over hot charcoal, turning regularly.
  3. Remove once kernels are golden brown and slightly charred.
  4. Rub with salt, chili, and lemon juice for extra flavor.
  5. Enjoy hot straight from the cob!
